== Description ==

= Collect. Curate. Publish. =

Tagembed is a social media feed aggregator plugin that helps you collect, curate, and embed social media feeds, Google Reviews, photos, videos, stories, and user-generated content (UGC) on your WordPress website automatically.
You can embed content from social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, X and review platforms such as Google Reviews. The plugin allows you to showcase user-generated content, social posts, and customer feedback in a customizable format.

== 4 Simple Step Process to Use Tagembed Social Media Feed Plugin ==

* **Collect:** Choose a source such as Instagram, Facebook, or Google Reviews
* **Customize:** Select layout and styling options

* **Publish:** Copy shortcode `[tagembed widgetid XXXXX]` and embed on your page

== Installation ==

Installation is quick and easy.

**Method 1: From WordPress Dashboard**

* Go to Plugins → Add New
* Search for “Tagembed”
* Click Install and Activate

**Method 2: Upload Plugin**

* Download plugin zip file
* Upload to `/wp-content/plugins/`
* Activate from dashboard

Tagembed is a social media aggregator platform that helps you collect, manage, and embed social media feeds and customer reviews on your website. You can display content from Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Google Reviews, Facebook, and more using customizable widgets.

Yes. Tagembed offers a free plan with limited features and paid plans with advanced customization, moderation, higher view limits, and branding removal.
= Which social media platforms does Tagembed support? =
Tagembed supports multiple social media and review platforms including Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, Twitter/X, Google Reviews, Yelp Reviews, and Airbnb Reviews.
= How can I connect my social media account? =
Connecting your account is simple. After installing the plugin, log in to your Tagembed account, choose your preferred platform, and follow the on-screen steps to connect your social media or review source.
= Can I display multiple social feeds on my website? =
Yes, Tagembed allows you to create and display multiple widgets from different social media platforms and review sources on your website.
= Can I hide unwanted posts from my feed? =
Yes. Tagembed provides moderation features that let you manually hide posts or automatically filter content using keywords and rules.
= Are Tagembed widgets mobile responsive? =
Yes, all Tagembed widgets are fully responsive and optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
= How do I embed social media feeds into my WordPress website? =
Install and activate the Tagembed plugin.
Create or log in to your Tagembed account.
Connect your preferred social media or review platform.
Customize your widget layout and design.
